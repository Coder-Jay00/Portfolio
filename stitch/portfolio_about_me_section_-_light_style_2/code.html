About

About Me.

I bridge the gap between complex engineering and human-centric design, believing that every pixel tells a story.

With over 8 years of experience in the digital space, I've transitioned from a visual artist to a full-stack developer. This dual perspective allows me to build applications that are not only high-performing but aesthetically pleasing and intuitive.

I specialize in building scalable frontend architectures and robust backend systems for startups and industry leaders. My approach is rooted in minimalism—eliminating the unnecessary so the meaningful can speak.

When I'm not pushing commits, I'm usually exploring the intersection of generative art and typography, or mentoring aspiring developers in the open-source community. My work is defined by a commitment to performance, accessibility, and visual harmony.
